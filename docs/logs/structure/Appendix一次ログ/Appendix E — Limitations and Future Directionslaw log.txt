Appendix E — Limitations and Future Directions

E.1 Limitations
This study attempts to observe, define, and evaluate External Semantic State Synchronization, 
but several limitations remain:
	• Model dependence
	• Inevitable long‑horizon Drift
	• Limits of external‑only observation
	• User‑dependent variability

E.2 Future Directions
	• Optimization of Seed design
	• Development of Drift prediction models
	• Cross‑platform generalization studies
	• Long‑term stabilization algorithms
